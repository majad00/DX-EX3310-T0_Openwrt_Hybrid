About This Custom Firmware

Welcome to the ultimate Dual-Band OpenWrt modification for the DX EX3310-T0 Zyxel router!
Firmware Credits

Modded by: majad qureshi at lut.fi

Zyxel Matrix projects are led by university students in embedding engineering from Lappeenranta and Vaasa, exclusively for educational purposes and are not official projects.
This firmware was specifically engineered to bypass standard OpenWrt limitations and unlock the raw power of the proprietary Econet EN751627.

Key Features Unlocked:

  • Support for Dual-Band architecture across all radios, including VDSL and VoIP
  • Direct integration with OEM EN751627 Management Information Base (MIB)
  • Customized Background Asynchronous Wi-Fi execution engine
  • Low-level root access to stock utilities, providing advanced functionality (without source code)
  • Option to flash stock firmware, allowing users to restore the original configuration of the router
  • Integrated LED , radio functionality, and a reset button within the OpenWrt ecosystem

"Why use the stock interface when you can build your own?"
